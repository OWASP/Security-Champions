Description
The organization has initiated pilot implementations of the security champions program within specific teams or departments. These pilots serve as a testing ground to validate the program's structure, engagement strategies, and impact before scaling more broadly. Pilots help gather insights, refine the approach, and build momentum through early successes.
Key indicators
▸
Active pilots running in selected areas of the organization,
▸
Defined scope and objectives for each pilot,
▸
Feedback mechanisms in place to evaluate pilot outcomes.
